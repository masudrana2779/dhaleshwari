About Us

Railroads transport goods

Railroads transport goods and people millions of miles every day. At Dhaleshwari Consultants, we believe in going the distance with them. Addressing aging infrastructure needs while continuing to move freight efficiently poses a global challenge.

Around the world, railroads continue to serve as a primary solution for effective hauling. Today’s industry priorities have become even more attuned to maximizing productivity and availability. Through fleet modernizations, track structure improvements and industry technology advancements, such as asset protection and predictive maintenance tools, railroads are taking efficiency, safety and reliability to the next level.

Railroad Track Material

OTM


BR&L supplies various OTM including joint bars, tie plates, track spikes, track bolts, gauge rods and rail anchors, as well as specialized fasteners. Our relationship with various manufacturers throughout the country enables us to pass savings along to you.

Building a network of suppliers within the rail industry takes years of service, and we have established contacts with the top manufacturers in the industry. When we deal in high volume, you enjoy the savings. Give us a chance to find exactly what you need to complete your next project.



Railroad Track Material

Relay Rail


Birmingham Rail & Locomotive Company, Inc. maintains inventories of relay rail and accessories for railroad track construction and maintenance

Your next project may benefit from substantial savings with quality relay rail for industrial sidings, spurs, plant track, mine haulage systems or temporary track. Relay material will provide years of dependable service at a lower cost than new rail. Our Birmingham or Houston facility can supply relay rail, joint bars, tie plates and other accessories to match existing track or for new installations.

Maintenance & Service


Our technicians do one thing… work on locomotives.

BR&L provides the most complete expertise in maintaining your locomotive with routine maintenance plans or service as needed. We can schedule your maintenance needs to include comprehensive 92 day inspections, or we can also tailor a schedule to meet your plans and budget.

Our services include wheel re-contouring, load box testing, engine repairs, air brake servicing, and electrical troubleshooting.

If your locomotive is down, your production is down. Keep your work on track with routine service from Birmingham Rail & Locomotive.



Repair & Rebuilds


Repair

When your locomotive is down, your production is down. BR&L specializes in repairs to keep you moving, and your work on track. Our technicians are qualified to diagnose and rectify issues in all modern or older industrial and main line locomotives. At your facility or in our shop, we can reduce down time and keep your locomotive running strong.

As part of our complete industrial locomotive service to you, we will also perform emergency repairs and routine maintenance on-site.

Rebuild

SWITCH OPERATIONS

Switch Material


We stock the hard to find switch material for quick repairs to existing turnouts. We also fabricate materials in-house to meet your specific need for quick turnarounds.

  • Switch Point Guard Rails: WCH FM Style, 755B or Design 90 for 75# to 140#, with or without gauge plate.
  • Switch Points: custom made from new, #1 relay rail, in either knife point or Samson point.
  • Switch Point Clips
  • Switch Point Stops
  • Switch Point Security Locks
  • Samson Stock Rails from new or #1 relay rail
  • Rods: Vertical and flat head and back rods for switches with open jaw clips or transit clips, either insulated or non-insulated.
  • Heel Blocks: all rail sizes, drillings in either stationary or floating.
  • Switch Plates Rigid braces and adjustable 811 Wedge or Pandrol design.
  • Gauge Plates: insulated or non-insulated, with rigid braces or Pandrol and Design 811 adjustable braces.
  • Switch Stands: New 51A, 22E, 36E and 36EH stands
  • Switch Stand repair parts
  • Connecting Rods: for all designs of switch stands and varying lengths
  • Switch Point Derails: single or double, complete
  • Hook Twin Turnout Plates for frogs and turnout plates
  • Compromise Bars: New cast and forged for many common and hard to find rail sections


Frogs


We stock a quality inventory of frogs to meet your demands.

Frogs: new self guarded solid manganese 100#RE, 100#RA, 110#RE, 115#RE, 132/136#RE; or reconditioned self-guarded solid manganese, railbound manganese insert, bolted rigid or flat top solid manganese 60# to 140#.

Frog Guard Rails: new or reconditioned, AREA design 504 or industrial design.



Turnouts


We manufacture turnouts to AREMA or individual industrial or railroad specifications. Turnouts may be new or reconditioned with slide plates with rigid braces or slide plates with Bethlehem Design 811 adjustable wedges.

Specifications required when ordering turnouts:

  • Rail Section & Drilling
  • Layout (left or right hand)
  • Frog Type
  • Frog Number
  • Brace Type
  • Guardrail type
  • Switch Length
  • Switch Point type
  • Insulation
  • Gauge
  • Switch Stand type
